== Description == 

Adds a "serpd" button to your blog posts so people can vote for you dynamically at serpd.com.  Allows custom configuration of button position on your blog.

== Installation ==

METHOD: Easy as Pie Automatic Addition to Your Blog
1. Upload `wp-serpd.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Done!  The SERPd button will appear on your posts automagically!
4. You can head on over to your "Options" ---> "serpd Options" panel and select a custom position if you'd like.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to be a member of SERPd to use this? =

No, you do not, although membership is free and allows you to "SERPd" your own posts.
